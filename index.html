About Me

I am a former postdoc in geometric analysis at the Differential Geometry Institute of the Leibniz Universität Hannover. I got my PhD at the Carl von Ossietzky Universität Oldenburg under the supervision of Prof. Dr. Boris Vertman.
My research focuses on the analysis of elliptic and parabolic PDEs on non-compact manifolds. In particular I focus on harmonic maps, minimal submanifolds, and geometric flows as well as their application in space-times.
